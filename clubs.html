Other Clubs At School Of India

Roots And Rhythms

The Dance and Music Club is where rhythm meets melody, and creativity takes center stage. It unites dancers, singers, and musicians who express themselves through movement and sound. From soulful tunes to energetic performances, every beat and step tells a story. Members explore different genres, learn new skills, and collaborate to create magic on stage. The club builds confidence, teamwork, and passion through practice and performance. Together, we celebrate the art of expression — where every note and move comes alive.

Art Pallete

The Art Palette Club is a haven for creativity, imagination, and self-expression. It brings together young artists who love to paint, sketch, and create unique works of art. Members explore various mediums — from traditional brushwork to modern digital art. Every session is a chance to experiment, learn, and share ideas with fellow creators. The club encourages individuality while fostering teamwork through collaborative projects. At Art Palette, every color tells a story, and every canvas reflects pure imagination.

MUN SOC

The MUN Society is where ideas, diplomacy, and debate come together. It encourages students to think critically, speak confidently, and understand global issues. Members take on the roles of world leaders, representing nations in simulated UN conferences. Through research and discussion, they learn the art of negotiation and problem-solving. The club builds awareness, leadership, and respect for diverse perspectives. At the MUN Society, every delegate’s voice matters — shaping tomorrow’s global thinkers.

Rotary

The Rotary Club inspires students to serve their community with compassion and leadership. It focuses on social welfare, teamwork, and making a positive difference through action. Members organize drives, awareness programs, and charity events to bring real change. The club promotes kindness, empathy, and responsibility toward society. Every project helps students grow as leaders with purpose and heart. At Rotary, service above self is more than a motto — it’s a way of life.

Environment

The Environment Club works to protect and preserve our planet through awareness and action. It teaches students the importance of sustainability and eco-friendly living. Members take part in clean-up drives, tree-planting, and conservation campaigns. The club encourages innovation in solving environmental challenges. Every effort, big or small, adds up to a greener tomorrow. Together, we nurture nature — because the Earth is our shared home.

Entrepreneurship

The Entrepreneurship Club sparks creativity, innovation, and business thinking in young minds. It gives students a platform to develop ideas, build startups, and learn leadership skills. Members explore marketing, finance, and teamwork through real-world projects. The club promotes problem-solving and confidence through hands-on experience. Every idea is seen as a chance to make a difference in the world. At the Entrepreneurship Club, imagination meets opportunity to create success.

Science

The Science Club ignites curiosity and a love for discovery among students. It encourages hands-on experiments, exploration, and learning beyond textbooks. Members dive into physics, chemistry, biology, and technology with creativity and passion. The club promotes scientific thinking and innovation through fun challenges and projects. It’s a place for questioning, testing, and understanding how the world works. At the Science Club, curiosity is the first step toward invention.
