Hi Everyone,

“

Roots and Rhythms is the Dance and Music Club that celebrates creativity through sound and motion. From classical melodies to modern beats, from graceful moves to high-energy performances — we create, perform, and express. The club brings together dancers, singers, and musicians who turn passion into performance on every stage.Through regular practice sessions, collaborations, and showcases, we aim to build confidence, teamwork, and originality among our members. Every performance is a reflection of dedication and love for talent — a space where rhythm becomes our voice and music connects us all.

”
