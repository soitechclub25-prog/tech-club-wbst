About Us

Welcome to the first-ever Tech Club of our school — a student-led initiative built to inspire curiosity, creativity, and innovation through technology. We explore a wide range of topics including coding, robotics, AI, gaming, and more. As pioneers of this new club, we aim to build a strong foundation for tech learning and collaboration. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and discussions, we create a space for students to develop practical skills and share ideas. Whether you're a beginner or already tech-savvy, the Tech Club is a place to grow, experiment, and shape the future—together.

Hamiz Elahi | Vice President

Hello Everyone,

I am Hamiz Elahi, and it gives me immense pleasure to introduce myself as the very first Vice President of School of India's very own Tech Club. I am a student of Class 9 and look forward to working with all my enthusiastic and innovative team members to have a wonderful year ahead. I would like to thank all my teachers for giving me this wonderful opportunity to be a part of this club. Technology has always been more than a passion for me — it has been a way of life.

I have been involved in technology ever since kindergarten, when my father taught me how to use basic software like MS Word, Notepad, and Paint. When he noticed my enthusiasm towards technology, he started teaching me how to code, and I wrote my very first Python program in 3rd grade.

Since then, I have learned a lot, including Python, HTML, CSS, Scratch, Arduino, and much more. I also have experience in robotics and have created projects such as a Wi-Fi controlled robot car and a line follower robot. In addition, I have gained experience in editing using different software like Adobe Photoshop, OpenShot, Krita, Kinemaster, and more. School of India has played a very important role in this journey of mine, and I am very grateful for the same.

This is your Tech Club Vice President signing off.

Warm Regards,
